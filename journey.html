Journey in Blockchain

Polygon Labs

Working on the Matic PoS chains' networking protocol, consensus systems, systems throughput optimizations, and scalability.
Worked on implementing a way to process transactions parallelly instead of serially to increase throughput.
Implemented support for EIP-4337 type of transactions in the PoS chain.

Carnegie Mellon University

Building ZUZlab, where we help individuals and businesses turn their reputation in a network into cash.
Worked under the guidance of Prof Seth Goldstein.
Using Hyperledger Fabric Blockchain and exploring various platforms such as NEAR, Solana, Algorand, etc.

Tech Mahindra

Created a cost-effective alternative to IBM Blockchain Platform to help developers create and handle customised blockchain networks.
Used Hyperledger Fabric, Python, Bash Script.

Tamil Nadu e-Governance

Created a decentralised Blockchain web-application to prevent cyber threats and unauthorised manipulations of government websites.
Got featured in the "Tamil Nadu Blockchain Policy 2020" which was released by the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu.
Used Hyperledger Fabric, NodeJS, ReactJS.
