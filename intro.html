Introduction

Pratik is a dedicated Blockchain Engineer at Polygon Labs, formerly known as Matic Network. He earned his degree from the esteemed Indian Institute of Technology, Bhilai, and has been actively involved in Blockchain Technology since late 2018.
While pursuing his undergraduate degree, Pratik took the initiative to co-found "Blockchain at IIT Bhilai" with the objective of facilitating students' understanding of blockchain technology and fostering their growth in this field. With a proven track record of working with prominent organizations and institutions, including Carnegie Mellon University, Tech Mahindra, and Tamil Nadu e-Governance Agency, Pratik has established himself as an accomplished professional in the Blockchain domain.

Currently residing in a quaint, remote town in India, Pratik shares his home with his parents and beloved pet dog.
